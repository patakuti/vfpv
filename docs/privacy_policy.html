Privacy Policy for VFPV

Last updated: June 9, 2026

VFPV ("the App") is a free-to-play FPV drone flight experience for Android, developed by patakuti.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Device Sensors

The App uses the device's accelerometer/gyroscope to detect tilt for flight control. This sensor data is used only locally within the App and is never transmitted.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/patakuti/vfpv
